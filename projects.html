Project Cars

This is my first project car, my 2002 BMW e46 325ci. This car started off its life as an automatic, but didn't stay that way for long once I got the car.

  • Manual swapped with a 5-speed from an e46
    • Lightened flywheel
    • Stronger clutch and pressure plate
  • Custom exhaust header and downpipe
  • GT35-clone turbocharger
  • Electronic boost control
  • Wideband O2 sensor
  • Custom MS43 (ECU) tuning
  • MAF from an Audi RS4
  • M50 manifold conversion
  • Freshly built engine
    • 3.0L assembly
    • New gaskets with a thicker headgasket with cutting rings
  • Welded differential for drifting (3.46 ratio)
